Pricing & Revenue Model

pricing-revenue v1.0.0 updated 2026-07-17 ↗Projection ◆Confidence: speculative raw JSON ↗
ℹREAD THIS FIRST

There are NO prices in this file, and that is deliberate. Pricing requires knowing the cost to serve (a BOM with zero real quotes), the value delivered (unproven), and willingness to pay (unmeasured). Inventing a subscription price now would produce a number that flows into the financial model and the funding ask and is wrong at the root. This file gives the pricing LOGIC and the inputs required, so a real price can be set the moment Phase 0 supplies them. A founder who says 'here is how we will price and here is what we need to know first' is more credible than one who presents a confident price built on nothing.

Pricing Philosophy

Principle

Price against value delivered, not cost incurred — but anchor on the value the customer can VERIFY, not the value we claim.

Why

The verifiable value (aeration savings, audit-labour savings) is a floor the customer will accept because they can check it. The claimed value (avoided mortality) is a ceiling we cannot prove. Price near the verifiable floor to win the deal, and let the unverifiable upside be the reason they renew once they have seen it.

The Anti Pattern

Pricing against avoided-mortality value would produce a high number justified by a claim we cannot substantiate, which collapses the first time a sophisticated buyer asks for evidence.

The Hardware Question

Should We Sell the Drone
Almost certainly not, at least initially.
Reasoning
  • A USD 20k+ capital ask to a fish farm is a non-starter on affordability and on gadget-fatigue.
  • Selling the hardware transfers maintenance and calibration responsibility to a customer who cannot do it, which destroys the data quality the whole product depends on.
  • Service retention keeps the data relationship, which is the moat.
  • We WANT the drones back in our docks being maintained by our people, because a fouled sensor in a customer's hands produces confidently wrong data that damages our reputation, not just theirs.
Model

AquaVision owns the hardware; the customer buys the service. Monitoring-as-infrastructure, not equipment sales.

Revenue Projection Status

Statement
No revenue projection appears here.
Why

It would require a price (unknown), a customer count (unknown), and a conversion rate (unknown), multiplied together into a number that would look authoritative and be meaningless. See financial-projections.json, which builds a SCENARIO STRUCTURE rather than a forecast, precisely so the assumptions are visible and attackable.

When This Gets Filled In

After Phase 0 supplies the cost, the aeration-spend anchor, and at least one paid-pilot WTP data point. Not before.
