Summary

Initial construction of the OmniDrone knowledge repository from the founding concept description.

Added
  • Full 14-section repository skeleton with manifest-driven index for webpage consumption.
  • Schema registry and data dictionary establishing the provenance discipline used throughout.
  • Problem space: taxonomy, stakeholder pain points, evidenced incident case studies, cost-of-inaction model.
  • Market: industry statistics sourced to FAO SOFIA 2024, TAM/SAM/SOM model, competitor teardown.
  • Hardware: airframe, probe winch, sensor payload, carriage/ejection, power budget, dock, indicative BOM.
  • Software: architecture, pipeline, model cards, digital twin specification, telemetry schema, API, dashboard spec.
  • Research corpus: thematic study collections across eight themes plus consolidated bibliography.
  • Operations, compliance, business model, impact, chart-ready datasets, and pitch/thesis kit.
Known Gaps
  • No field trial data exists. Every performance figure is a design target or a projection, tagged as such.
  • Bibliography entries derived from search-engine summaries carry verification_status = cited_from_search_result and MUST be opened and checked before thesis submission. Do not submit a reference list you have not read.
  • Financial projections are unvalidated models built on stated assumptions. The assumptions are listed so they can be attacked; attack them.
  • Regulatory analysis reflects a reading of publicly summarised CAAP rules as of mid-2026 and is not legal advice. Confirm with CAAP directly before any BVLOS commitment appears in an investor deck.
  • Competitor pricing is largely absent from public sources and is marked unknown rather than guessed.
Decisions Recorded
  • Chose to leave traction.json deliberately empty rather than populate it with invented customers. A pitch with honest zeros and a credible plan survives diligence; a pitch with invented traction does not.
  • Chose in-situ probe + multispectral fusion as the core scientific claim, rather than imagery alone, because uncalibrated indices do not produce defensible time series.
  • Chose to treat biosecurity cross-contamination as a first-class hardware requirement rather than an operational footnote, because it is the strongest technical objection to the multi-site concept.

Maintenance Policy

On Edit

Bump the file's own `version` and `last_updated`, and add an entry here. A repository whose changelog lags its content is a repository nobody trusts.

On New Claim

Any new quantitative claim requires a provenance tag and, if verified_external, a bibliography entry. No exceptions — this is the rule that keeps the thesis defensible.

On Data Arrival

When real data replaces synthetic data, remove the illustrative_synthetic tag in the same commit. A stale synthetic tag on real data is nearly as damaging as the reverse.
