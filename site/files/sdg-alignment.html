UN Sustainable Development Goal Alignment

sdg-alignment v1.0.0 updated 2026-07-17 ?Assumption raw JSON ↗

Note

SDG alignment is easy to overclaim — every startup maps itself to half the goals. This file distinguishes goals the product GENUINELY and DIRECTLY serves from ones it touches tangentially, and it flags where the product's second-order effects could run AGAINST a goal. An honest SDG map includes the tensions.

Primary Alignment

Zero Hunger

Sdg
2
Target
2.4 — sustainable food production systems and resilient agricultural practices
How Directly
strong
Mechanism

Aquatic food is a major and growing protein source; reducing losses and improving yield in aquaculture directly increases food availability. Aquaculture is now the majority of aquatic animal production.

Citations
fao-sofia-2024
Honest Caveat

The contribution is real but bounded by our addressable fraction of the sector and by whether farms act on the information. Do not claim to 'solve hunger'.

Life Below Water

Sdg
14
Target

14.2 — sustainably manage and protect marine and coastal ecosystems; 14.1 — reduce marine pollution

How Directly
strong
Mechanism

Measuring aquaculture's effluent load and supporting carrying-capacity management reduces the eutrophication of shared water bodies. Pollution detection supports 14.1.

Honest Caveat

The strongest environmental claim, BUT it depends on the carrying-capacity use case actually being adopted, which requires cross-farm data-sharing that is unresolved (see ethics-data-privacy.json). And note the over-stocking paradox below — the product could also enable intensification.

Responsible Consumption and Production

Sdg
12
Target
12.2 — sustainable management and efficient use of natural resources
How Directly
moderate-strong
Mechanism

Reducing feed waste (feed is ~60 percent of operating cost and a major environmental load) improves resource efficiency. Precision dosing reduces chemical and antimicrobial use.

Citations
innovasea-fcr-guide
Honest Caveat

The antimicrobial claim cuts both ways — easier dosing can mean more dosing. See the tension below.

Secondary Alignment

No Poverty

Sdg
1
How Directly
moderate
Mechanism

For smallholders, a single kill event can be financially catastrophic. Preventing or mitigating it protects livelihoods. Continuous records could unlock credit and insurance for the currently uninsurable.

Honest Caveat

Directly limited by the affordability paradox — the smallholders most exposed to poverty are the ones who cannot pay for the product. The poverty contribution depends entirely on the cooperative and institutional channels succeeding.

Citations
worldbank-disease-risk-2014

Decent Work and Economic Growth

Sdg
8
How Directly
moderate
Mechanism

Removes the dangerous pre-dawn boat run (a real drowning risk), and stabilises aquaculture incomes. SDG 8.8 — safe working environments.

Honest Note
The occupational-safety angle is genuine and underused. It is a human argument, not just an efficiency one.

9

moderate

Brings precision-agriculture-grade instrumentation to a sector and geography that lacks it.

Climate Action

Sdg
13
How Directly
weak-moderate
Mechanism

Increasing frequency of thermal and rainfall extremes worsens fish kills; the platform shortens reaction time to climate-driven events. Adaptation, not mitigation.

The Tensions An Honest Sdg Map Must Include

Note

This section is what separates a real SDG analysis from a marketing exercise. Every one of these is a way the product could work AGAINST a goal it claims to serve.

Tensions
Tension
Over-stocking (against SDG 14)
Detail

Better instruments may let operators push stocking density higher, worsening the eutrophication and carrying-capacity problems the product claims to help. A tool sold on environmental stewardship could enable environmental degradation.

Our Response

Carrying-capacity analytics aimed at collective governance; not marketing as a licence to intensify. Partial mitigation only. See risk R-15.

Tension
Antimicrobial use (against SDG 3, Good Health)
Detail

Easier dosing can mean more prophylactic antimicrobial use, contributing to antimicrobial resistance — a serious public health problem.

Our Response

Dose ceilings, human authorisation, precision placement to reduce total dose. Weak controls against a determined operator. See ethics-data-privacy.json.

Tension
Equity (against SDG 10, Reduced Inequalities)
Detail

If the product only reaches well-capitalised commercial farms, it widens the gap between them and smallholders — the opposite of reducing inequality. Precision technology that only the rich can afford entrenches advantage.

Our Response

Cooperative and institutional channels are the mechanism for equitable reach, and they must not be allowed to slip. This is an active commitment, not an automatic outcome.

Tension
Privacy and surveillance (against multiple goals)
Detail

Aerial imagery over communities, and the latent surveillance capability, could harm the people the product is meant to serve.

Our Response
No person-detection, no enforcement use, data-minimisation. See ethics-data-privacy.json.
Tension
Environmental footprint of the hardware (against SDG 12)
Detail

Lithium batteries, electronic waste, and the risk of a ditched drone contaminating a food-production water body are real environmental costs of the solution itself.

Our Response

Lithium containment, ditching provision, and honest lifecycle accounting. A product sold on stewardship must account for its own footprint. See environmental-impact.json.

Why This Matters

An SDG map with only benefits is a sustainability-washing exercise, and sophisticated impact investors and thesis committees recognise it instantly. Including the tensions — with honest, sometimes-partial responses — is what makes the alignment credible.

How To Use This

For Impact Investors

Lead with SDG 2, 14, and 12 (the genuine primary alignments), then volunteer the tensions. An impact investor who sees you have thought about the over-stocking paradox trusts the rest of your claims.

For the Thesis

This is the significance-of-the-study section. The tensions belong in the discussion chapter as limitations, not hidden.

The Discipline

Never map the product to a goal it does not directly serve just to fill the grid. Three strong alignments plus honest tensions beats seventeen weak ones.
