Value Proposition

value-proposition v2.0.0 updated 2026-07-18 ↗Projection ◆Confidence: low raw JSON ↗

How To Read This File

Value at AquaVision is not one proposition, it is three, layered by phase and audience. Phase 1 sells to beginners on free education, guidance, and organization. Phase 2 sells to growing farms on ROI-positive analytics, marketplace access, and expert time. Phase 3 sells to commercial and enterprise operators on the hardware value prop this repository originally led with: earlier detection, avoided mass mortality, and aeration savings. Each phase keeps its own value hierarchy ranked by how easily a sceptical buyer can verify the claim — the discipline that made the original hardware pitch credible is applied here to the software claims too, not relaxed for them.

The Strategic Conclusion

Sell tier 1 and 2. Earn tier 3. Prove tier 4. Never promise tier 5 before the data exists. This inverts the instinct of every founder in this category — everyone leads with the dead fish — and it is why most of them do not survive diligence.

Differentiation
Against Doing Nothing
A metered reduction in an existing electricity bill. Not a story about fish kills.
Against A Fixed Buoy

Depth and coverage. And the honest admission that below a scale threshold, the buoy wins and we will say so.

Against A USV

Transit speed across many stations, simultaneous aerial imaging, and the ability to ignore dikes, nets, and cage structure entirely. Their endurance beats us and always will.

Against Satellite

Resolution, revisit rate, cloud immunity, and depth. And we should ingest their data rather than compete with it.

Against Salmon Platforms
Cost structure and tropical design case. Not technology — we would lose that fight.
Against A Generic Drone Service

The probe. Anyone can fly a camera over a lake; calibrated depth-resolved chemistry from a hover is genuinely hard.

The Honest Summary

What We Can Defensibly Say Today

A phased platform where Phase 1 is a free app with no users yet, Phase 2 is a monetisation layer that cannot exist meaningfully before Phase 1 does, and Phase 3 is a hardware upgrade designed to profile water at depth across many stations at low marginal labour cost, with its own calibration ground truth on board, in an industry where nobody currently measures anything and fish die because of it.

What We Cannot Say

That any of the three phases works yet. Phase 1 has not launched. Phase 2 has no data to run on. Phase 3 has not flown.

The Pitch That Survives Diligence

Not 'we prevent fish kills' and not 'we have thousands of users.' Rather: three honestly-staged claims — free tools that should be checkably useful to a beginner from day one; a paid layer whose ROI a growing farm can verify against its own numbers once it exists; and a documented, recurring, expensive hardware-solvable failure mode at the top of the market, with the one thing we have to prove about each layer named plainly, and the boring, checkable value in each phase that pays its own way while the exciting claims are still being proven.
