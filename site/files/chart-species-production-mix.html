Where Farmed Aquatic Animals Are Produced

By region and by system, 2022

chart-species-production-mix ✓Sourced raw JSON ↗
fao-sofia-2024

Two facts that justify the beachhead, at every phase: 70 percent of production is in Asia, and 62.6 percent of farmed aquatic animals come from inland systems — precisely the environment of both the Phase 1/2 smallholder/commercial software user base and the Phase 3 OmniDrone hardware layer. Building for tropical inland Asian aquaculture is building for the centre of the industry, whether the product reaching that farmer is a free app or a drone.

Notes

Percentages sum to 99 for the regional series due to FAO rounding. Do not silently normalise to 100 — show the rounding honestly.
