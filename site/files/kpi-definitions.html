KPI Definitions

kpi-definitions v1.0.0 updated 2026-07-17 raw JSON ↗

Every KPI the system and the business track, with a PRECISE operational definition. Conforms to schema-registry.json#/definitions/kpi. The definition is the point — if two people can compute different numbers from it, it is not finished.

The Discipline

Each KPI has a `status`: measured (from real data), modeled (from a projection), target_only (a goal with no baseline), or awaiting_data (defined but empty). At TRL 3, almost everything is target_only or awaiting_data — and saying so is the honest thing. A dashboard of confident KPIs with no data behind them is theatre.

Phase Note

This file now spans two different products. `phase_1_2_saas_kpis` below are the metrics for the free/premium software platform (acquisition, activation, conversion, marketplace, subscription retention). Every other section (`operational_kpis` through `impact_kpis`) is the Phase 3 hardware/monitoring layer — OmniDrone, docks, sondes, PondTwin. The Phase 1 app has zero real users as of this writing, so every Phase 1/2 KPI below is `awaiting_data`, exactly as honestly empty as the Phase 3 KPIs are. Do not let the software story's earlier launch date create pressure to backfill these with invented numbers.

Phase 1 2 Saas Kpis

Monthly Active Users (MAU) kpi-mau
⌀Awaiting data
count(distinct user_id where last_active_at within 30 days)
↑ higher is better · count
Distinct registered users who perform at least one tracked in-app action (open a pond profile, log a reading, view a learning module, post in community) within a rolling 30-day window.

The core Phase 1 acquisition-funnel metric. No app exists in production yet, so this is a definition, not a number.

DAU/MAU Ratio (Stickiness) kpi-dau-mau-ratio
⌀Awaiting data
avg(daily distinct active users) / MAU
↑ higher is better · ratio
Daily active users divided by monthly active users, averaged over a calendar month.

Distinguishes an app farmers check daily (feeding, logging) from one they open once and abandon. A weak ratio would be an early signal the free tier is not habitual.

Activation Rate kpi-activation-rate
⌀Awaiting data
activated_signups_within_7d / total_signups
↑ higher is better · %
Fraction of new signups who complete a defined 'activated' action set (farm profile created + at least one water-quality or feeding log entered) within 7 days of registration.

The single most important Phase 1 funnel metric — a signup who never activates was never really acquired. Precise definition matters: 'created an account' is not activation.

Free-to-Paid Conversion Rate kpi-free-to-paid-conversion
⌀Awaiting data
converted_users_within_90d / activated_users_in_cohort
↑ higher is better · %
Fraction of activated free-tier users who upgrade to a paid Premium subscription within a defined observation window (target: 90 days from activation).

THE monetization-thesis metric — the entire freemium business model reduces to this number times MAU times price. Nobody has paid for anything yet; treat every assumed value as fiction until a real cohort exists.

Marketplace Gross Merchandise Value (GMV) kpi-marketplace-gmv
⌀Awaiting data
sum(transaction_value) over period
↑ higher is better · currency
Total value of feed/equipment/buyer transactions conducted through the in-app marketplace in a period, before AquaVision's take-rate is deducted.

The marketplace does not exist yet. This KPI is pre-defined so that, when it launches, take-rate revenue is computed consistently against a documented GMV figure rather than an ad hoc one.

Expert Consultation Bookings kpi-consultation-bookings
⌀Awaiting data
count(completed_consultation_bookings)
↑ higher is better · count
Count of paid aquaculturist/vet consultation sessions booked and completed through the platform in a period.

Phase 2 revenue line distinct from subscription and marketplace take-rate.

Premium Subscription Churn kpi-subscription-churn
⌀Awaiting data
lapsed_subscribers_in_period / paying_subscribers_at_period_start
↓ lower is better · %
Fraction of paying Premium subscribers who cancel or lapse (non-renewal) per billing period.

The SaaS-layer counterpart to kpi-churn (below), which is the Phase 3 dock/hardware-service churn metric — keep the two separate; they measure different products and will likely have very different values.

Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) kpi-cac-organic-vs-paid
⌀Awaiting data
acquisition_spend_in_channel / activated_users_from_channel
↓ lower is better · currency
Fully-loaded cost (content, community, paid channels if any) to acquire one activated free-tier user, split by organic/community-led vs paid-channel acquisition.

Go-to-market for Phase 1 is explicitly organic/community-led to keep this low; this KPI is how that claim gets checked rather than assumed. See go-to-market.json.

Operational Kpis

Survival Rate kpi-survival-rate
⌀Awaiting data
harvested_count / stocked_count
↑ higher is better · %
Proportion of stocked individuals harvested alive, per unit per cycle.

The headline outcome a farmer actually cares about. Our value claim ultimately reduces to moving this number. Sourced from operator harvest records, not measured by us.

Feed Conversion Ratio kpi-fcr
⌀Awaiting data
feed_supplied_kg / biomass_gain_kg
↓ lower is better · ratioband 1 – 2.5 ratio
Mass of feed supplied divided by biomass gain, per unit per cycle.
innovasea-fcr-guide
Diel DO Minimum kpi-do-minimum
⌀Awaiting data
↑ higher is better · mg/Lband 3 – ∞ mg/L
The lowest dissolved oxygen recorded at a unit in a 24h period, at the shallowest cage-relevant depth.

The number nobody currently has, because it occurs pre-dawn. THE flagship measurement.

Diel DO Amplitude kpi-do-amplitude
⌀Awaiting data
do_max - do_min over 24h
⇅ band is better · mg/L
Difference between the daily DO maximum and minimum at a unit.

A large amplitude signals a large algal standing crop and therefore a large nightly oxygen demand — a leading indicator a single daily reading cannot produce.

Stratification Strength kpi-stratification-strength
⌀Awaiting data
⇅ band is better · dimensionless
Schmidt stability index normalised by column depth.
idso-schmidt-stability-1973
Hypolimnetic Oxygen Debt kpi-oxygen-debt
⌀Awaiting data
↓ lower is better · mg/L*m
Depth-integrated oxygen deficit below the thermocline.

Quantifies the size of the bad-water reservoir waiting to be delivered by mixing.

Aeration Runtime kpi-aeration-hours
⌀Awaiting data
↓ lower is better · hours
Total aerator operating hours per unit per period.

The VERIFIABLE ROI anchor. Metered, already budgeted, checkable in one cycle. Lead with this. See value-proposition.json#/the_value_hierarchy.

System Health Kpis

Sortie Success Rate kpi-sortie-success
◎ 98%target — no baseline
↑ higher is better · %
Completed sorties / attempted sorties, excluding weather scrubs.
Seasonal Availability kpi-availability-seasonal
◎Target only
↑ higher is better · %
Fraction of scheduled sorties actually flown, reported PER SEASON not annually.

MUST be reported seasonally. Availability is lowest in the monsoon, which is when risk is highest. An annual average hides exactly the gap that matters. See mission-profiles.json#/environmental_envelope.

Data Completeness kpi-data-completeness
◎Target only
↑ higher is better · %
Fraction of expected measurements that were captured, QC-passed, and stored.
Sensor Drift kpi-sensor-drift
◎Target only
↓ lower is better · channel-specific
Deviation of the sonde against the laboratory reference at the weekly check, per channel.

The metric that catches silent fouling. See risk R-01.

Retrieval Calibration Quality kpi-calibration-quality
⌀Awaiting data
↑ higher is better · R² / µg/L
R² and RMSE of the site-and-day-local NDCI-to-chlorophyll regression.

Often fitted on 3-4 points, so often poor. This KPI exists to make that honesty visible rather than hidden.

Product Health Kpis

Alert-to-Action Rate kpi-alert-action-rate
⌀Awaiting data
↑ higher is better · %
Fraction of dispatched alerts that led to a logged operator action within a defined window.

A FIRST-CLASS product health metric. If it falls, people have stopped trusting the alerts and the product has failed regardless of sensor accuracy. This is the alert-fatigue early-warning signal. See data-pipeline.json.

False Alarm Rate kpi-false-alarm-rate
⌀Awaiting data
↓ lower is better · %
Fraction of alerts a human reviewer marked as not warranting the alert.

Drives alert fatigue. Tune models for precision to keep this low, accepting lower recall.

Anomaly Detection Precision kpi-detection-precision
⌀Awaiting data
↑ higher is better · %
True positives / (true positives + false positives), on human-reviewed detections.

Precision is prioritised over recall here. See ai-ml-models.json.

Business Kpis

Stations per Dock kpi-stations-per-dock
⌀Awaiting data
↑ higher is better · count
Active sampling stations served by one dock.

The dominant driver of unit economics. See unit-economics.json.

Contribution Margin per Dock kpi-contribution-margin-dock
⌀Awaiting data
↑ higher is better · currency
Revenue per dock minus cost-to-serve per dock, per period.

Cannot be computed until real BOM and maintenance costs and a real price exist. Deliberately empty rather than modeled.

Maintenance Labour per Dock kpi-maintenance-labour-dock
⌀Awaiting data
↓ lower is better · hours
Field-labour hours per dock per period.

The most-underestimated cost line. See risk R-14.

Customer Churn kpi-churn
⌀Awaiting data
↓ lower is better · %
Fraction of customers not renewing per period.

Phase 3 hardware/monitoring-service churn specifically — commercial/enterprise customers renting dock capacity. Expected low once trust is established (data lock-in, hardware in our docks), but unproven. Do not confuse with kpi-subscription-churn (phase_1_2_saas_kpis), which is the Premium software-subscription churn — a different customer base and likely a different rate.

Impact Kpis

Mortality Events Mitigated kpi-loss-events-avoided
⌀Awaiting data
↑ higher is better · count
Documented cases where a warning led to an intervention that a farmer judged prevented or reduced a loss.

A COUNTERFACTUAL and therefore hard to measure honestly. Requires the methodology in methodology.json. Never claim a prevented kill without the evidence structure to support it.

Dangerous Boat Trips Avoided kpi-dangerous-trips-avoided
⌀Awaiting data
↑ higher is better · count
Pre-dawn/bad-weather manual monitoring trips replaced by autonomous sorties.

The occupational-safety impact. Concrete and measurable.

Smallholder Reach kpi-smallholder-reach
⌀Awaiting data
↑ higher is better · %
Smallholder operators served (via cooperatives/programmes) as a fraction of total served.

The equity metric. Measures whether the mission is being served or quietly abandoned in favour of the payers. See social-economic-impact.json.

The Meta Point

Statement

Almost every KPI here is status awaiting_data or target_only — the Phase 1/2 SaaS KPIs included, not just the Phase 3 hardware ones.

Why That Is Correct

At TRL 3 there is no real hardware data, and the Phase 1 app has zero real users, so honest KPIs are empty on both sides of the business. A dashboard showing confident values for either set would be fabricating them. The value of this file today is that it DEFINES each metric precisely, so that the moment real data arrives — whether that is the first app signup or the first drone sortie — everyone computes the same number, and nobody can quietly redefine a KPI to make it look better. Pre-committing the definitions is a discipline, not a limitation.
