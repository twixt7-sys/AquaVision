Pitch Deck Outline

pitch-deck-outline v1.0.0 updated 2026-07-17 ◎Target raw JSON ↗

Note

A slide-by-slide outline for the investor deck, pulling from across the repository. Built on the wager stated throughout: rigorous honesty is more fundable than polished optimism, for the right investor. Each slide names its source file and the ONE thing it must accomplish.

Deck Philosophy

Principle

Lead with the inflection, sell the checkable ROI, volunteer the risks, and never overclaim the predictive capability.

The Arc

This industry just became the majority of the world's aquatic animal production → its operators are blind to the thing that kills their stock → the blindness is economic, not technical → an aerial platform inverts that economics → here is the one thing we must prove and how we'll prove it cheaply → here is the boring ROI that pays for it meanwhile.

What This Deck Refuses To Do
  • Show a hockey-stick revenue forecast
  • Claim we prevent fish kills before validation
  • Hide the BVLOS dependency
  • Invent traction
  • Imply TRL beyond 3
