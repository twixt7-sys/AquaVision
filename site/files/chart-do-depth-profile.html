The Loaded Gun: What a Vertical Profile Reveals

Dissolved oxygen and temperature versus depth, pre-dawn

chart-do-depth-profile ⚠SAMPLE DATA — NOT REAL raw JSON ↗
seafdec-laguna-fishkill idso-schmidt-stability-1973
⚠SYNTHETIC WARNING

FABRICATED but physically plausible profile. Describes no real station. Illustrates the stratification mechanism. Webpage MUST badge as illustrative_synthetic.

A stratified water column with a survivable surface (3.1 mg/L) hiding anoxic, sulfide-generating bottom water (0.2 mg/L, negative ORP) below the thermocline at ~3.5 m. A surface sensor sees nothing wrong. A cold rain that mixes this column delivers the bottom water to the fish — the exact mechanism in the Laguna de Bay fish-kill literature. Depth is where the killing happens, and only a profile can see it.

Notes

This is the chart that justifies the reeled probe over every surface-only competitor. It should sit next to the diel chart in the pitch: one shows time-blindness, the other depth-blindness. Together they are the case for the platform.
