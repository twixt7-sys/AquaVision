Spend Plan by Company Phase — Not a Revenue Forecast

Milestone-gated use of funds across the software launch (Phase 1/2) and the hardware validation track (Phase 3 sub-stages 3.0-3.4)

chart-financial-projection ↗Projection raw JSON ↗

This is deliberately a SPEND plan gated by de-risking milestones, NOT a hockey-stick revenue projection, now covering BOTH tracks of the phased business. Phase 1 (free app) and Phase 2 (premium/marketplace) are comparatively cheap, software-only stages whose milestone is user traction and conversion, not physical validation. Phase 3 (OmniDrone/IoT/PondTwin) is the capital-intensive hardware track — internally staged 3.0 through 3.4 (Definition through Commercial pilot), which is the SAME TRL-gated engineering sequence this chart described before the pivot, simply relabelled to sit inside Phase 3 rather than describing the whole company. At TRL 3 hardware with no price, no cost, and no customer on either track, a revenue forecast would be fiction. What can be honestly shown is the shape of the spend and the milestone each stage unlocks. A serious investor prefers this to a fabricated up-and-to-the-right curve.

The Message

Fund de-risking on both tracks, not a forecast. Phase 1/2 de-risks acquisition and monetization; Phase 3's sub-stages de-risk the hardware, exactly as before the pivot. Phase 3.0 can even trigger an honest stop. See financial-projections.json and funding-ask.json — both still need a pass to formally adopt this phased structure; treat the Phase 1/2 entries here as reasonable modeled_projection placeholders pending that update, not as a finalized budget. When real revenue scenarios exist (post-Phase 2 traction and post-Phase 3.4 pilot), render them as a three-scenario band (pessimistic/base/optimistic) per track, never as a single confident line.

Revenue Projection Status

DELIBERATELY ABSENT for both tracks. See financial-projections.json#/what_investors_should_take_from_this_file.
