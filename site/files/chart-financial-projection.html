Spend Plan by Phase — Not a Revenue Forecast

Milestone-gated use of funds through validation

chart-financial-projection ↗Projection raw JSON ↗

This is deliberately a SPEND plan gated by de-risking milestones, NOT a hockey-stick revenue projection. At TRL 3 with no price, no cost, and no customer, a revenue forecast would be fiction. What can be honestly shown is the shape of the spend — cheap first, expensive validation season — and the milestone each phase unlocks. A serious investor prefers this to a fabricated up-and-to-the-right curve.

The Message

Fund de-risking, not a forecast. Each phase removes a specific risk; Phase 0 can even trigger an honest stop. See financial-projections.json and funding-ask.json. When real revenue scenarios exist (post-Phase 4), render them as a three-scenario band (pessimistic/base/optimistic), never as a single confident line.

Revenue Projection Status

DELIBERATELY ABSENT. See financial-projections.json#/what_investors_should_take_from_this_file.
