Team Structure & Capability Map

team-structure v2.0.0 updated 2026-07-18 ?Assumption raw JSON ↗
ℹIMPORTANT

This file describes the roles this venture REQUIRES, not people it has. Every `holder` field is deliberately null. Fill them with real names and real, verifiable backgrounds. Do not let generated placeholder biographies reach an investor — fabricated team credentials are the fastest way to destroy a fundraise and, depending on jurisdiction, a securities problem.

Capability Requirements

Chief Executive / Commercial Lead

Role Id
ceo
Holder
—
Why the Venture Needs It

Aquaculture technology does not sell itself, at any phase. Someone has to earn a beginner farmer's trust in a free app with no track record in Phase 1, earn a paid subscription in Phase 2, and later sit on a dike at 5 a.m. with a commercial operator who has been burned by a previous gadget vendor to sell Phase 3 hardware.

Critical Skills
  • Aquaculture sector relationships
  • Fundraising
  • Regulatory navigation with CAAP and BFAR
  • Pricing against loss avoidance and farmer outcomes rather than feature lists across all three phases
Failure Mode If Absent

A technically excellent product that nobody uses or buys, because the team optimised the OmniDrone instead of app adoption and the conversion funnel.

Chief Technology Officer / Systems Architect

Role Id
cto
Holder
—
Why the Venture Needs It

This is a hard systems integration problem across three phases that must share one data model — a mobile app that works offline for smallholders, an ML/subscription layer built on the data that app produces, and eventually an aerial vehicle, wet sensing, dispensing, edge inference, and a digital twin. Someone must own the whole envelope so Phase 3 doesn't require rebuilding what Phase 1 already shipped.

Critical Skills
  • Software and mobile systems architecture
  • UAS systems engineering (for Phase 3)
  • Mass and power budgeting (for Phase 3)
  • Sensor integration
  • The discipline to say no to features that break either the app's offline-first constraints or the drone's payload fraction
Failure Mode If Absent

A Phase 1 app whose data model has to be thrown away to support Phase 3 hardware, or — the classic hardware-first failure — four drone subsystems that each work and a vehicle that cannot fly with all of them attached.

Aquaculture / Limnology Scientist

Role Id
aquaculture_scientist
Holder
—
Why the Venture Needs It

The product's claims are scientific claims. Without domain science in-house, the company will confidently ship a chlorophyll index it believes is a toxin detector.

Critical Skills
  • Limnology and water column dynamics
  • Fish physiology and stress response
  • Field sampling protocol design
  • Validation study design
Failure Mode If Absent

Overclaiming. The company's entire moat is trustworthiness of measurement, and this role is the person who defends it internally against the sales pressure to just say the score predicts kills.

Note

In the Philippine context, SEAFDEC/AQD, UP Visayas, and state universities with fisheries programmes are the obvious recruiting and collaboration grounds.

Remote Sensing / Machine Learning Lead

Role Id
rs_ml_lead
Phase

Phase 2 (predictive health models on Phase 1 data) through Phase 3 (remote sensing retrieval)

Holder
—
Why the Venture Needs It

Radiometric calibration, glint handling, and inland-water retrieval are specialist skills. A generalist ML engineer will produce a model that performs beautifully on the training lake and collapses on the next one.

Critical Skills
  • Radiometric calibration
  • Inland water optical retrieval
  • Computer vision for small-object detection over water
  • Model calibration and uncertainty quantification — not just accuracy
Failure Mode If Absent

Uncalibrated indices presented as measurements, which produces a time series that looks like science and is not.

Embedded / Flight Systems Engineer

Role Id
embedded_lead
Phase
Phase 3
Holder
—
Critical Skills
  • Flight control integration
  • Failsafe logic — especially water-aware failsafes
  • Edge compute integration
  • Real-time telemetry and link management
Failure Mode If Absent

A vehicle whose link-loss behaviour was designed for land and which therefore ditches in a lake the first time a squall rolls in.

Mechanical / Mechatronics Engineer

Role Id
mech_lead
Phase
Phase 3
Holder
—
Why the Venture Needs It

The winch is the hardest mechanism on the vehicle and the most novel. It must pay out and recover a wet, fouling, snagging sensor from a moving hover in wind, thousands of times, without a person present.

Critical Skills
  • Winch and tether mechanism design
  • Marine-grade sealing and corrosion
  • Metered dispensing mechanisms
  • Design for field maintenance by non-engineers
Failure Mode If Absent

A winch that works in the lab and jams over water, which is an unrecoverable failure with a drone on the far side of a lake.

Platform / Full-Stack Engineer

Role Id
software_lead
Holder
—
Why the Venture Needs It

The Phase 1 app is the whole company until it isn't. Someone must ship and operate it — offline-tolerant, low-end-Android-friendly, cheap to run at thousands of free users — before there is any Phase 2 revenue to fund a bigger team.

Critical Skills
  • Mobile and web app engineering for low-connectivity, low-end-device conditions
  • Time-series data infrastructure
  • Offline-tolerant sync
  • Dashboard and twin visualisation (Phase 3)
  • API design
Failure Mode If Absent

A cloud platform that assumes connectivity and devices that rural smallholder farmers do not have, which quietly caps Phase 1 acquisition regardless of how good the content and AI assistant are.

Head of Product — App & Premium Tier

Role Id
product_lead
Holder
—
Why the Venture Needs It

Phase 1 succeeds or fails on whether a beginner farmer with no prior app experience can set up a pond, log water quality, and come back tomorrow. That is a product design problem, not a features list, and it is a different discipline from the systems engineering the CTO owns.

Critical Skills
  • Mobile product design for low-literacy and low-connectivity users
  • Onboarding and activation design
  • Freemium-to-paid funnel design for Phase 2
  • Usability research with smallholder farmers, not just commercial operators
Failure Mode If Absent

An app that is technically correct and that beginner farmers abandon after one session, which starves the whole platform of the Phase 1 data that Phase 2 and Phase 3 both depend on.

Growth & Community Marketing Lead

Role Id
growth_marketing_lead
Holder
—
Why the Venture Needs It

A free app does not acquire thousands of smallholder users by existing. Someone must own organic, community-led distribution through fish-farming groups, cooperatives, and extension networks, at a CAC low enough that the free tier is actually a funnel and not just a cost centre.

Critical Skills
  • Community and content-led growth
  • Relationships with farmer cooperatives and extension networks
  • Basic funnel analytics (activation, retention, free-to-paid conversion)
  • Local-language content production
Failure Mode If Absent

A well-built app with no users, which makes the entire phased thesis moot — there is no Phase 1 data to build Phase 2 models on, and no acquisition funnel to convert into Phase 2 subscribers.

Holder
—
Why the Venture Needs It

The business model depends on a BVLOS authorisation the company does not have. Someone must own that pathway from day one, because it has a long lead time and it gates the unit economics.

Critical Skills

  • Safety case construction

A company that discovers in year three that its entire margin assumed an operating mode it is not permitted to use.

Advisory Needs

Domain
Limnology of Philippine lakes
Rationale

Site-specific water column behaviour is not generalisable from temperate literature. Taal, Laguna de Bay, Buhi, and Sampaloc each behave differently. Matters most once Phase 3 sensing exists, but informs what Phase 1 asks farmers to log.

Domain
Aquaculture extension and cooperatives
Rationale

Phase 1 distribution to smallholders runs through cooperatives and extension networks, not through paid acquisition channels.

Domain
Civil aviation regulation
Rationale
The Phase 3 BVLOS pathway.
Domain
Veterinary / aquatic animal health
Rationale

Disease-identification guidance in the Phase 1 app and anything involving medicine dispensing in Phase 3 both cross into regulated veterinary territory. See regulatory-framework.json.

Domain
Agricultural finance
Rationale

Smallholders cannot buy capital equipment outright. The Phase 3 financing structure is as much of a product decision as the hardware, and it is worth designing early even though it is years out.

Domain
Mobile growth and freemium SaaS
Rationale

The Phase 1-to-Phase 2 conversion funnel is a standard freemium problem this team has not solved before; an advisor who has run one elsewhere is cheaper than learning it by trial and error on real farmers.

Cultural Commitments

  • The scientist has a veto on public claims. If the science lead says the model is not ready, it does not ship, and the commercial lead does not get to overrule that.
  • Field time is mandatory for engineers, at every phase. An engineer who has never watched a beginner farmer struggle through app onboarding will design a Phase 1 flow nobody finishes, and one who has never stood on a fish cage at dawn will design a Phase 3 pond that does not exist.
  • Post-mortems on lost aircraft, and on failed app releases or growth experiments, are blameless and published internally. A team that hides its failures learns nothing and repeats them.
