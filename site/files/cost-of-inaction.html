Cost of Inaction — Loss Model

cost-of-inaction v1.0.0 updated 2026-07-17 ↗Projection ◆Confidence: low raw JSON ↗
ℹREAD THIS FIRST

This is a MODEL, not a measurement. Every number below is either a sourced external statistic (tagged) or an assumption the authors invented (tagged). Its purpose is to make the loss structure explicit and attackable — not to prove a market. If you present this as evidence of demand, you will be caught, because the assumptions are visible and several of them are shaky. The correct use is: 'here is our model, here are the assumptions, tell us which one you think is wrong.' That framing wins meetings. 'Our research shows PHP X million' loses them.

The Contradiction We Must Resolve

Three single events in this list total roughly PHP 58 million plus 343 tonnes. The twenty-year national recorded total is PHP 322 million. These cannot be reconciled unless the national figure counts only a narrow subset of officially investigated incidents. Either the national figure is a severe undercount, or the event figures are overstated by reporting. We do not know which, and until we do, neither number should anchor a business case. Resolving this by obtaining BFAR's primary records is a Phase 0 task and a genuinely publishable contribution.

Provenance
ƒCalculated
Addressable By Omnidrone
partially
Honest Addressability

We can shorten detection time. We cannot make a farmer destock, and we cannot manufacture emergency aeration capacity that does not exist. On a farm with aerators and a manager willing to run them, an early warning has real value. On a smallholder cage with neither, the warning converts an unexpected loss into an expected one — which is worth something psychologically and nothing financially. The addressable fraction is therefore a function of the customer's response capacity, not of our detection performance. This is the most important sentence in this file.

Chronic Sub-Lethal Production Loss

Category Id
chronic-sublethal
Description

Fish that survive but grow slowly, convert feed poorly, and die at elevated background rates because water quality sits chronically at the margin.

Visibility
near zero — nobody reports this because nobody measures it
Hypothesis

This category is larger in aggregate than catastrophic mortality, possibly by an order of magnitude, and it is completely invisible in every published loss statistic.

Reasoning

A kill event is a discrete, reported, dramatic loss. A 12 percent reduction in growth rate across an entire cycle across an entire sector is silent, is absorbed into the expected yield, and is never attributed to anything. Farmers do not experience it as a loss; they experience it as normal.

Provenance
?Assumption
Confidence
speculative
Why This Matters Enormously

If this hypothesis is true, the OmniDrone's value proposition is misstated throughout this repository. We are pitching catastrophe insurance, when the real product might be continuous yield optimisation — a different message, a different customer conversation, a different pricing model, and a far better business, because it pays on every cycle rather than on the rare bad one. Catastrophe is a more emotionally compelling pitch; chronic loss may be the more valuable and more defensible one.

How To Test It

Phase 3's longitudinal deployment can measure it directly: correlate continuous water quality against realised FCR and survival across a full cycle, on farms with no kill events. If the correlation is strong, restructure the whole pitch around it.

Addressable By Omnidrone
strongly — this is exactly what continuous profiling is for
Intellectual Note

This is the most important untested claim in the entire repository. It is flagged as speculative because it is, but it deserves the team's attention far more than another decimal place on the mortality figures.

Self-Inflicted Water Degradation

Category Id
reflexive-water-degradation
Description

The uneaten feed from the category above decomposes, consumes oxygen, and releases ammonia — degrading the water that then causes the losses in the categories above.

Citations
fao-nutrient-dynamics-eutrophic ija-environmental-impacts-ph
Provenance
✓Sourced
The Structural Insight

This is a feedback loop, and it is the most intellectually interesting thing in the problem space. Overfeeding costs money AND degrades water AND therefore increases mortality AND stresses fish so they convert feed worse AND therefore requires more feed. The loop is vicious and it closes.

Why It Matters For the Pitch

It means a single intervention — feeding on evidence rather than on schedule — attacks the largest cost line and the primary kill mechanism simultaneously. That is not a feature list; that is a coherent thesis about the system's dynamics, and it is what elevates this from 'a drone with sensors' to a considered argument. Put this on a slide as a loop diagram. It is the best slide available to this company.

Addressable By Omnidrone
this is the core claim

Brutal Honesty

Almost none of this is ours. Disease losses are driven by pathogen introduction, broodstock quality, and biosecurity — not by observability. We detect the consequences earlier and we flag stress windows. Those are real but modest contributions. This category belongs in the problem picture because it sizes the sector's pain; it does NOT belong in our revenue model, and putting it there would be the single most dishonest move available in this pitch. Resist it.

Aeration Energy Waste

Description

Aerators run continuously because nobody knows when they are needed, or run too late because nobody knew they were.

Visibility

Addressable By Omnidrone
strongly and demonstrably
Why This Is the Sleeper

This is the easiest ROI in the entire product and it is buried at position six in this file. Aeration electricity is a large, monthly, already-metered cost. A pilot that shows a farm ran its aerators 30 percent fewer hours with equal or better DO outcomes is a complete, self-contained, verifiable business case that requires no belief in our mortality claims at all. It can be demonstrated in one cycle. It should probably be the lead ROI in the first commercial pitch, precisely because it is boring and checkable.

labour-and-safety
Addressable By Omnidrone

Note

The monetary value is modest — a technician's hours. The human value is not. Pre-dawn small-boat operation in monsoon weather is a genuine drowning risk that the industry treats as routine. This category should never be reduced to a labour cost line; it is an argument that stands on its own and it lands with cooperatives, LGUs, and development funders in a way that FCR arithmetic does not.

Category Id

Description
Fifteen years of operation producing zero retained knowledge about the farm's own water.
Visibility

Addressable By Omnidrone
completely
Note

Unquantifiable and real. Every cycle begins with the same ignorance as the first. The sector cannot build the predictive models that would help it because the training data was never kept. This is the argument for the platform as infrastructure rather than as a gadget — and it is the argument that justifies a subscription rather than a hardware sale, because the value compounds with duration.

What This File Deliberately Does Not Contain

Statement

There is no bottom-line 'total addressable loss' number here, and its absence is intentional.

Why

Constructing one would require multiplying our sourced figures by our invented assumptions, producing a large, authoritative-looking number whose error bars span an order of magnitude. That number would then be repeated in the deck, in the thesis, and eventually back to us by an investor who had checked it. The temptation to produce it is strong and it should be resisted until the assumptions in the register above have been tested against real farms. When they have been, this file becomes genuinely powerful. Until then, the honest deliverable is the loss STRUCTURE, not a loss total.
