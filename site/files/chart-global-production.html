The Inflection: Aquaculture Overtakes Capture Fisheries

Global aquatic animal production, 2022

chart-global-production ✓Sourced raw JSON ↗
fao-sofia-2024

In 2022, for the first time, aquaculture produced more aquatic animals than capture fisheries — 51 percent of the total. This is the structural shift that makes aquaculture technology a category — both the Phase 1/2 software-management layer (a market sized by operator count, not tonnage) and the Phase 3 hardware-monitoring layer. Capture fisheries cannot be sensor-optimised or app-managed; farmed systems can.

Notes

Capture figure derived: aquaculture 94.4 Mt at 51% implies total aquatic animals ~185.4 Mt (stated by FAO), so capture ~91.0 Mt. The precise capture split should be confirmed against SOFIA's own breakdown before use. This is the single most important chart in the pitch — use it once, early.
