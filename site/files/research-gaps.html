Research Gaps & Open Questions

research-gaps v1.0.0 updated 2026-07-17 ƒCalculated raw JSON ↗

Purpose

Everything this repository does not know, ranked by consequence. This is the file a thesis committee should read first and the file a founder should reread monthly.

The Ranking Principle

Ranked by 'if this answer comes back wrong, how much of the plan dies?' — not by how interesting the question is. The most consequential gaps are usually the most boring ones.

Tier 1 Existential

Description

If any of these resolves badly, the venture as conceived does not work. All are answerable in Phase 0 with phone calls and arithmetic — none require research.

Gaps

gap-response-capacity

Question
Will a farmer act on a warning, or merely watch the loss arrive with better information?
Why Existential

The entire venture assumes information changes behaviour. If a warning arrives at 3 a.m. and the farmer has no affordable response available, the product informs people about their own losses in real time. That is not a business.

Current Status
UNTESTED. Assumed throughout every file in this repository.
How To Resolve

Phase 0 discovery. Ask twenty-five operators: 'if you had known twelve hours earlier, what would you have done — concretely, with what equipment?' The answers decide the product.

Cost To Resolve
Two weeks of conversations.
Flagged In
  • problem-statement.json
  • cost-of-inaction.json
  • customer-personas.json

gap-payload-budget

Question
Can the aircraft carry the mission at all?
Why Existential

The paper mass budget in omnidrone-specifications.json DOES NOT CLOSE with all payloads fitted — 6.56 kg against a 7 kg target, leaving no dispensing capacity.

Current Status

PARTIALLY RESOLVED, and the resolution changed the product. Per-mission payload configuration is required, and dispensing must be repositioned as precision treatment rather than bulk feeding.

The Remaining Unknown

The component masses are invented. A real sonde may weigh double the 0.55 kg assumed, in which case the budget breaks again.

How To Resolve

Call a sonde vendor. Ask what their lightest unit with optical DO and fluorometric chlorophyll weighs. One phone call resolves the largest single uncertainty in the hardware.

Cost To Resolve
One afternoon.

gap-bvlos

Question
Will CAAP authorise BVLOS, and on what timeline?
Why Existential

The flagship use case — the 04:30 dawn sweep — requires either BVLOS or a human at the dock before dawn. Without it, the diel-bias argument that justifies the whole platform is unreachable, and the unit economics collapse.

Current Status

UNKNOWN. Our entire regulatory reading comes from secondary summaries of CAAP rules found via search. We have not spoken to CAAP.

How To Resolve
Talk to CAAP. In Phase 0, with nothing to ask for.
Cost To Resolve
A meeting. It has a long lead time, which is why it must start now rather than in Phase 4.

gap-willingness-to-pay

Question
Is there any price at which the target customer will pay?
Why Existential

Most target operators currently spend approximately zero on water quality monitoring. We are not taking share from a budget line; we are trying to create one — a fundamentally harder task.

Current Status
UNKNOWN. Market sizing is blocked on it.
How To Resolve

Not a survey — stated willingness to pay in agricultural surveys is notoriously inflated. The only reliable measurement is an invoice someone actually paid.

Cost To Resolve
A paid pilot, eventually. Meanwhile, ask what they already pay for and to whom.

gap-winch-viability

Question

Can a winch deploy and recover a sonde from a hover, reliably, unattended, in a cage array?

Why Existential

It is the only defensible thing about the product. Without it we are a camera drone with no position.

Current Status
UNKNOWN. The pendulum control problem and the snagging problem are both unresolved.
How To Resolve

Phase 1 gantry testing, then Phase 2 flight. But FIRST: a systematic literature search on UAV-tethered sondes. Somebody has almost certainly hit this wall and their published failure is worth more than our own would be.

Cost To Resolve
The search is a week. The testing is a phase.

Tier 2 Shapes the Product

Description
These do not kill the venture but they change what it is.
Gaps

gap-chronic-vs-catastrophic

Question
Is chronic sub-lethal loss larger in aggregate than catastrophic mortality?
Why It Matters Enormously

If yes, the entire value proposition is misstated throughout this repository. We are pitching catastrophe insurance when the real product may be continuous yield optimisation — a different message, a different customer conversation, a different pricing model, and a far better business, because it pays on every cycle rather than on the rare bad one.

The Secondary Prize

It is also a far more tractable machine learning problem: continuous outcomes, abundant data, every cycle a sample. Catastrophe prediction has a rare-event problem that may be insurmountable. If this hypothesis is right, it solves the business AND the ML at once.

Current Status
SPECULATIVE. Flagged as the most important untested claim in the repository.
How To Resolve

Phase 3 longitudinal correlation: continuous water quality against realised FCR and survival on farms with NO kill events.

Flagged In
  • cost-of-inaction.json
  • ai-ml-models.json
Note

This gap deserves more attention than it is getting. It is the difference between a good business and a great one, and nobody is looking at it.

gap-weather-availability

Question
What fraction of the monsoon season is actually flyable?
Why It Matters

We cannot fly in the storms that trigger overturn events, and we cannot charge in extended overcast. Our availability is lowest exactly when risk is highest, for two independent reasons.

The Implication

An honest reading says the OmniDrone is a coverage instrument for CHRONIC management, not a catastrophe-prevention system. That is a smaller, less thrilling story and it is probably the true one.

Current Status

UNQUANTIFIED. Any availability figure quoted to a customer must be seasonal, not annual, or it is misleading.

How To Resolve

Historical weather data against the flight envelope. This is a desk exercise available today and nobody has done it.

gap-buoy-hybrid

Question
Should the product include fixed buoys?
Why It Matters

FOUR independent lines of reasoning in this repository point at a buoy hybrid: the competitive matrix says the hybrid dominates either alone; the weather gap says we need continuity we cannot provide; the HAB collection notes fixed devices can carry plankton imaging we cannot; and the dock's solar limitation compounds the availability problem.

Current Status
Noted four times, acted on zero times.
The Observation

When four unrelated analyses converge on the same conclusion, that is a finding, not a coincidence. This should stop being a footnote and start driving the roadmap.

How To Resolve
An architecture decision, not research. Make it.

gap-scale-threshold

Question
Below what operation size does a fixed buoy simply beat us?
Why It Matters

It sets the SAM, the pricing, and the sales qualification criteria all at once. Selling below the threshold means extracting money for a worse solution.

Current Status
Qualitative only.
How To Resolve
Arithmetic, once real costs and real farm layouts are known. Phase 0.

gap-dispensing-doses

Question
What are the real dose masses of the treatments farms actually use?
Why It Matters
We are designing a hopper without knowing what goes in it.
Current Status
UNKNOWN.
How To Resolve
Ask five farms what they apply, how much, and how often. A conversation, not research.

Tier 3 Evidence Gaps

Description
Things the literature does not establish that our argument needs.
Gaps

gap-counterfactual

Question
Does earlier detection actually change outcomes?
Why It Matters

No published study documents a farm that had monitoring and avoided a kill. That comparison is the actual evidence a rational buyer wants, and nobody has produced it.

Current Status
ABSENT FROM THE LITERATURE ENTIRELY.
The Opportunity

Producing it would be a genuine scientific contribution and a strong thesis objective in its own right — quite apart from its commercial value.

How To Resolve

A matched comparison: farms with and without monitoring, same lake, same season. Hard, but publishable.

gap-philippine-statistics

Question
How many aquaculture operations are there, of what size, where?
Why It Matters

Every Philippine market number in this repository is a guess without it. Market sizing is blocked.

Current Status
The BFAR Philippine Fisheries Profile has this. We have not obtained it.
How To Resolve

A records request. This is the single highest-value missing datum in the repository and it is obtainable with a phone call.

gap-loss-statistics-contradiction

Question
Why do Philippine fish kill loss figures not reconcile?
Why It Matters

The PHP 322.1 M twenty-year national figure and the PHP 43.13 M single-event figure cannot both be right unless the national figure counts only a narrow subset. Until resolved, neither number should anchor a business case.

Current Status
CONTRADICTION DOCUMENTED, UNRESOLVED.
How To Resolve
BFAR primary incident records. Resolving it would itself be publishable.

gap-species-thresholds

Question

What are the actual DO and ammonia tolerance thresholds for Philippine tilapia and milkfish, by life stage?

Why It Matters

THE HIGHEST-CONSEQUENCE PLACEHOLDER IN THE REPOSITORY. Our alert thresholds currently rest on general aquaculture practice. An incorrect threshold becomes a false 'all clear' in production — the system tells a farmer their fish are fine and they are not.

Current Status

PLACEHOLDER. industry-statistics.json says 'unknown — populate from primary source' and nobody has.

How To Resolve

Primary literature search. This is a week of work and it is currently gating a safety-critical function.

gap-feed-cost-transferability

Question

Does the US-derived '60 percent of operating cost' feed figure transfer to Philippine tilapia and milkfish?

Why It Matters

It anchors the entire ROI argument and it is currently sourced to a vendor blog citing NOAA.

Current Status
ASSUMED, not established.
How To Resolve
SEAFDEC/AQD has Philippine cost structures. Ask them.

gap-monitoring-labour-claim

Question
Is it actually true that farmers do not monitor because the labour costs too much?
Why It Matters

This is the reframe at the intellectual centre of the whole project — that the bottleneck is the marginal labour cost of an observation. It is structural reasoning, not a cited finding. Nobody has published it.

Current Status

The weakest link in the literature review's argument, and it is the link everything else hangs from.

How To Resolve

Establish it empirically in Phase 0 discovery. Doing so would convert the project's central premise from plausible to demonstrated — and would be a contribution in itself.

Tier 4 the Gap About Our Gaps

Gaps

gap-adoption-literature

Question

Why is the socioeconomic adoption collection nearly empty when adoption is the most likely failure mode?

The Finding

This repository has eight research themes. Seven are populated with studies about sensors, algorithms, twins, and fish. The one about whether anyone will adopt, pay for, and keep using the technology has four entries, all of which argue against the venture.

Why It Is Diagnostic

The team's instinct was to research the technology, not the adoption. That instinct is precisely why aquaculture technology has a poor adoption record despite good technology. We went where the light was.

Current Status
ACKNOWLEDGED IN studies-socioeconomic-adoption.json AND NOT YET CORRECTED.
How To Resolve

Two weeks reading smallholder technology adoption literature. It is more likely to change the plan — and save the money — than any amount of winch engineering.

Priority

This is the most actionable finding in the entire research corpus, and it is a finding about us rather than about the field.

gap-prior-art-search

Question
Has anyone already built a UAV-tethered sonde and failed?
Why It Matters

We claim novelty in the integration. 'Nobody has done X' is the weakest possible claim in a literature review and it usually means 'we have not found it yet'.

Current Status
NOT SEARCHED. Flagged in three separate files as required and never done.
How To Resolve

A dedicated systematic search on UAV-tethered sensors, winched deployment from rotorcraft, and adaptive aerial sampling. A week. Before Phase 1, not after.

The Stakes

If somebody hit the pendulum wall and published it, that paper is worth more to us than a month of our own flight testing.

gap-verification

Question
Are our citations real?
The State

ZERO sources in the bibliography have been opened and verified. Twenty-five are cited from search-result summaries; twenty need verification outright. One (ai-aqua-review-2024) is not a real citation at all and must be fixed or removed.

Why It Matters

A committee member who finds one fabricated citation will disbelieve the entire bibliography, and they will be right to.

How To Resolve

Open every source. The priority queue is in references-bibliography.json#/bibliography_health.

The Meta Observation

Statement

Almost every Tier 1 gap is resolvable in Phase 0 with phone calls and arithmetic. None require a laboratory, a prototype, or a grant.

The List
  • Call a sonde vendor — resolves the mass budget.
  • Call CAAP — resolves the regulatory pathway.
  • Request BFAR statistics — unblocks market sizing.
  • Talk to twenty-five farmers — resolves response capacity and willingness to pay.
  • Run a literature search — resolves the prior art question.
  • Read the weather data — resolves seasonal availability.
The Point

Six weeks of unglamorous work would resolve more risk than a year of engineering. The temptation will be to build the winch first, because building is more satisfying than asking. That temptation is the thing most likely to kill this company, and it is worth naming plainly here so that it can be recognised when it arrives.
