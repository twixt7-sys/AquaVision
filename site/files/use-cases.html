Use Cases

use-cases v1.1.0 updated 2026-07-18 ◎Target raw JSON ↗

Note

Each use case traces to a problem id in problem-taxonomy.json and generates requirements in the hardware and software files. A use case that traces to nothing is a feature someone wanted.

Phase Scope Note

AquaVision is a phased platform. uc-01 through uc-10 below describe the Phase 3 hardware layer (OmniDrone, IoT probes, PondTwin) and predate the phased restructuring, but their content is unchanged and still valid — they are simply now scoped explicitly to Phase 3, sold to commercial and enterprise operators. uc-11 and uc-12 describe Phase 1 and Phase 2 respectively: the free app used by a beginner with no sensors at all, and the premium layer a growing farm subscribes to once it has outgrown manual tracking. Together the twelve use cases span the whole funnel, from a smallholder's first pond to an enterprise deployment.

Use Cases

The Pre-Dawn Profile

Id
uc-01-dawn-profile
Phase
3
Priority
must
Traces To
  • prob-diel-sampling-bias
  • prob-blindness-at-depth
  • wq-do-depletion
Actor
System (autonomous), overseen by farm manager
Trigger
Scheduled, 04:30 local
Preconditions
  • Dock has charge
  • Weather within envelope
  • Airspace clear
Flow
  • Dock opens, drone launches on the stored route.
  • Transits to station 1, establishes a stable hover at profiling altitude.
  • Winch pays out; sonde descends at controlled rate, sampling continuously.
  • Descends to configured depth or until bottom proximity is detected.
  • Recovers, transits to station 2. Repeats across the array.
  • Returns to dock. Probe is rinsed. Data uploads.
  • Platform computes the diel minimum, the thermocline depth, and the oxygen debt.
  • If any threshold is breached, an alert goes to the manager's phone.
Postconditions
  • Profiles stored
  • Dashboard updated
  • Alerts dispatched if triggered
Value Delivered

The number nobody has ever had: what the dissolved oxygen actually was at the daily minimum, at depth, at every cage. This is the single most valuable sortie the system flies and it is the one a human will never do reliably.

Why This Is Use Case One

Because the entire diel-bias argument reduces to this flight. A system that cannot fly at 04:30 unattended has not solved the problem it was built for — it has only made the 9 a.m. reading more convenient.

Failure Modes
  • Dock fails to open in rain
  • Wind exceeds envelope precisely during the season of greatest risk
  • Probe fouls and drifts without failing loudly
Honest Note
Requires BVLOS or a pilot willing to be at the dock at 04:30. Without BVLOS authorisation, this use case — the flagship one — is either illegal or expensive. That dependency is the sharpest reason BVLOS sits on the critical path.

Algal Bloom Mapping and Ground-Truthing

Id
uc-02-bloom-mapping
Phase
3
Priority
must
Traces To
  • bio-hab
  • prob-hab
Actor
System, initiated by schedule or by satellite/dashboard trigger
Flow
  • Multispectral survey grid flown at constrained solar angle to control glint.
  • NDWI applied first to mask non-water pixels.
  • NDCI computed per water pixel, producing a relative chlorophyll field.
  • System identifies the index extremes and plans probe stations at high, medium, and low index locations.
  • Probe drops at each, measuring in-situ fluorometric chlorophyll-a and phycocyanin.
  • Local regression fits index to concentration for this site, this day, this illumination.
  • The whole map is converted from relative index to estimated concentration with a stated confidence interval.
  • If phycocyanin is elevated alongside chlorophyll, the system flags cyanobacterial suspicion and recommends laboratory sampling.
Value Delivered

A concentration map of the entire water body, calibrated by measurements taken minutes earlier by the same machine — rather than an uncalibrated index that cannot be compared to yesterday's.

Citations
mishra-ndci-2012 mcfeeters-ndwi-1996 mdpi-hab-meta-2021 sciencedirect-uav-multispectral-ponds-2024
Boundary

The output is biomass, not toxicity. The system recommends sampling; it never declares water safe or unsafe. People eat the shellfish and that boundary is not negotiable.

Why It Matters

This is the architectural insight made operational — the probe calibrating the camera, in one sortie. It is the use case to demo.

Stratification and Overturn Watch

Id
uc-03-stratification-watch
Phase
3
Priority
must
Traces To
  • wq-stratification
  • wq-overturn
  • prob-blindness-at-depth
Flow
  • Repeated profiles across days build a time series of thermocline depth and Schmidt stability.
  • Near-bottom DO and ORP quantify the hypolimnetic oxygen debt — the size of the bad water waiting below.
  • Weather forecast is ingested: incoming cold fronts, heavy rain, and wind events are the triggers.
  • When a strongly stratified column with a large oxygen debt meets a forecast mixing event, the system raises an advisory.
  • Advisory names the mechanism in plain language, not a score: 'the bottom three metres are anoxic and rain is forecast tonight; mixing would bring that water to your cages.'
Value Delivered

Visibility of the specific mechanism identified in the Laguna de Bay research as the cause of mass kills — DO depletion with ammonia and hydrogen sulfide — days before it fires.

Citations
seafdec-laguna-fishkill idso-schmidt-stability-1973
Honest Note
This is the use case the whole product exists for, and it is the one we cannot yet deliver. The composite risk score is unvalidated. Until Phase 3, the system can SHOW the stratification and the oxygen debt — which is already more than anyone has — but it must not claim to predict the kill. Showing the mechanism and letting an experienced farmer draw the conclusion is both honest and, given their expertise, probably better.
The Bitter Irony

The mixing event we are warning about is usually a storm. We cannot fly in the storm. So the system's job is to have measured the loaded gun in the days beforehand, not to observe the trigger being pulled. That is a real capability and it should be described in exactly those terms.

Surface Mortality and Distress Scan

Id
uc-04-mortality-scan
Phase
3
Priority
must
Traces To
  • bio-mortality-detection
  • bio-stress-behaviour
  • prob-disease-detection
Flow
  • RGB survey over cage array at detection altitude.
  • CV model detects floating mortalities and surface-piping behaviour.
  • Detections carry calibrated confidence and are geolocated to a specific cage.
  • Manager receives an alert with the image crop attached — never a bare classification.
  • Human confirms or rejects. The rejection is training data.
Value Delivered

Mortality found in a morning sortie rather than at the next manual round, and localised to a cage rather than to a farm.

Boundary

Floating mortalities only. In species whose dead fish sink, the majority of mortality is invisible to this use case, which caps the claim substantially and must be stated when selling it.

Note

Surface piping is the higher-value detection of the two. It is a strong, visually obvious hypoxia signal and it means the fish are in trouble RIGHT NOW — which is actionable in a way that counting corpses is not.

Condition-Aware Feed Dispensing

Id
uc-05-precision-feeding
Phase
3
Priority
should
Traces To
  • op-feed-waste
  • op-feed-distribution
  • prob-feed-waste
Flow
  • Before a feed run, the system checks current DO and temperature at the cages.
  • If DO is below the feeding threshold, the system recommends deferring — stressed fish do not convert feed, and feeding into low oxygen adds organic load to water that is already short.
  • If conditions permit, the drone dispenses the metered ration across a planned pattern rather than dumping at one point.
  • Dispensed mass and coordinates are logged against the cage record.
Value Delivered

Feed applied when the fish can use it and spread where they are. Attacks the largest cost line and the primary kill mechanism with one action.

Citations
innovasea-fcr-guide ussec-fcr-power
The Loop

This is the reflexive insight from cost-of-inaction.json made operational: overfeeding wastes money AND degrades water AND stresses fish AND worsens conversion AND requires more feed. Feeding on evidence breaks the loop at its cheapest point.

Honest Limitation

We infer feeding appetite from water condition. Incumbent systems watch the fish eat with an underwater camera, which is a direct observation and strictly better feedback. A knowledgeable buyer will point this out and they will be right. Our answer is coverage — we do this across forty cages, they do it in the one cage with the camera.

Targeted Conditioner or Treatment Application

Id
uc-06-targeted-treatment
Phase
3
Priority
should
Traces To
  • prob-treatment-delivery
  • wq-ph-swing
Flow
  • Twin identifies a spatial hotspot — a low-pH region, a low-DO pocket.
  • Operator reviews the recommendation, the dose, and the coverage area.
  • Operator authorises. This step is not skippable.
  • Drone dispenses to the specified coordinates and logs actual delivered mass and position.
  • Follow-up profile measures the effect. The loop closes.
Value Delivered

Treatment goes where the problem is instead of across the whole pond, which cuts chemical use and cost. And nobody puts a boat out at 4 a.m. in bad weather to do it.

Safety Gate

Human authorisation is mandatory and hard-coded. An autonomous system that doses a water body on its own judgement is one model error away from poisoning a harvest, and the feature would demo beautifully right up until it did.

Regulatory Note

Anything beyond conditioners and lime — specifically veterinary medicines — crosses into regulated territory. See regulatory-framework.json before assuming this use case is legal for a given substance.

Post-Typhoon Rapid Damage Assessment

Id
uc-07-post-typhoon
Phase
3
Priority
could
Traces To
  • env-typhoon-damage
  • op-cage-maintenance
Flow
  • After the storm passes and conditions return to the flight envelope, a survey grid is flown over the array.
  • Imagery identifies displaced cages, collapsed structure, torn nets above the waterline, and debris fields.
  • Profiles check for salinity shock and post-storm mixing effects.
  • Report generated for the operator and, if relevant, the insurer.
Value Delivered

Hours instead of days to know what survived, and nobody has to walk on damaged structure over deep water to find out.

Why It Is Underrated

Philippine-specific, emotionally concrete, and it opens an insurance-adjacent customer relationship. It is also the one use case where our weather limitation is irrelevant, because by definition we fly afterwards.

Pollution Event Detection

Id
uc-08-pollution-detection
Phase
3
Priority
could
Traces To
  • env-oil-spill
  • env-industrial-effluent
  • env-solid-waste
Flow
  • Routine survey imagery is screened for oil sheen, foam, unusual colour, and debris.
  • Detection triggers a probe drop at the plume edge and centre.
  • Operator is notified with imagery and chemistry.
Boundary

Detects the plume and the anomaly. Does not identify the compound and cannot attribute it to a source. Attribution is a legal question our data can support and not settle — and overstating that in front of a farmer who then accuses a neighbour is how a company acquires a lawsuit.

Id
uc-09-audit-record
Phase
3
Priority
should
Traces To
  • econ-cert-compliance
  • prob-data-fragmentation
Flow
  • Continuous logging accumulates against the site and unit records.
  • At audit time, the operator exports a date-ranged environmental report.
  • Report includes QC flags and provenance, so an auditor can see which readings are trustworthy.
Value Delivered
Eliminates a task the farm currently pays someone hours to do by hand.
Why It Matters Commercially

Lowest-risk claim in the product and a genuine wedge into commercial farms. It requires no belief in anything we model — it is just logging done properly.

Lake-Scale Carrying Capacity Assessment

Id
uc-10-carrying-capacity
Phase
3
Priority
could
Traces To
  • op-stocking-density
  • wq-eutrophication
  • env-fish-kill
Actor
Cooperative, LGU, or BFAR
Flow
  • Profiles aggregated across multiple operators' units over a season.
  • Spatial and temporal patterns of oxygen demand mapped against stocking.
  • Evidence base assembled for a carrying capacity discussion.
Value Delivered

The first quantitative basis for a decision that is currently made politically or not at all.

The Tension

This use case requires multi-farm data and directly collides with farmer data sensitivity. The operator who benefits from the lake-wide analysis is the one who least wants their own stocking visible in it. Unresolved. See ethics-data-privacy.json.

Note

Potentially the highest public value of the whole platform and the hardest to actually deliver, because it is a governance problem wearing a technology costume.

A Beginner Logs Water Quality by Hand and Gets AI Feeding Advice

Id
uc-11-beginner-manual-logging
Phase
1
Priority
must
Traces To
  • prob-data-fragmentation
  • prob-feed-waste
  • prob-disease-detection
Actor
Smallholder farmer, using the free AquaVision App
Trigger
Farmer opens the app during or after a pond visit
Preconditions
  • Farmer has a smartphone and the app installed
  • No sensors, probes, or drones are involved anywhere in this use case
Flow
  • Farmer measures what they can — often just observation, sometimes a cheap test strip or kit — and enters readings manually, or takes a photo of the pond and fish for the AI to assess.
  • App records the entry against the farm's setup profile: species, stocking density, pond size, days into cycle.
  • AI farming guide compares the entry against the feeding schedule and husbandry guidance for this species and stage.
  • If the entry or photo suggests a problem — cloudy water, fish at the surface, visible lesions — the app surfaces a disease-ID opinion and a plain-language suggestion.
  • Farmer can post the same photo to the community for a second, human opinion.
  • Entry is stored, building the farm's own history for future feeding schedule and record-keeping features.
Postconditions
  • Log entry stored
  • Feeding schedule updated or confirmed
  • Community post created if the farmer chose to ask
Value Delivered

The same discipline of logging that the Phase 3 hardware automates for a commercial operator, made available for free to someone who has never had it at all — without needing a single sensor, probe, or drone.

Why This Matters For the Platform

This is the data-accumulation engine described in the platform overview. Every manual entry is a data point AquaVision would otherwise have to buy or simulate, and it is also the on-ramp that eventually converts this farmer into a Phase 2 subscriber once the farm outgrows manual tracking.

Honest Note
The app has not launched. Nobody has entered a real log yet. The AI farming guide and photo-based disease ID are design targets trained toward literature and public data, not yet validated against real farmer photos. Accuracy claims are `assumption` provenance until a real evaluation exists.

A Growing Farm Subscribes to Premium Analytics and Buys Feed via the Marketplace

Id
uc-12-growing-farm-premium-marketplace
Phase
2
Priority
must
Traces To
  • prob-data-fragmentation
  • op-feed-waste
  • econ-cert-compliance
Actor
Owner-operator of a small-commercial farm, upgrading from the free App
Trigger

Farm has accumulated a season or more of Phase 1 logs and manual tracking is no longer keeping up

Preconditions
  • Farmer has an existing Phase 1 App account with logged history
  • Farmer subscribes to AquaVision Premium
Flow
  • Farmer subscribes; premium analytics dashboard unlocks and processes the farm's existing Phase 1 log history.
  • Dashboard surfaces trends the farmer had not noticed by hand — a feeding pattern correlated with slower growth, a recurring dip in a logged parameter before past problems.
  • Smart recommendations suggest an adjustment; the farmer decides whether to act on it.
  • Farmer browses the marketplace for feed and equipment, compares listed prices against the local supplier, and places an order.
  • Farmer books a paid consultation with a vet or aquaculturist through the platform when a question exceeds what the AI guide or community can answer.
  • At certification audit time, the farmer exports the accumulated environmental log as a compliance record.
Postconditions
  • Subscription active
  • Marketplace order placed
  • Consultation booked or completed
  • Audit export generated
Value Delivered

A paid layer whose ROI a farmer can check against numbers they already have — a marketplace price against a known local price, a consultation against a known travel cost — rather than a promise that requires trusting AquaVision's modelling.

Why This Matters For the Platform

This is the Phase 2 monetisation event the whole funnel is built to produce, and it depends entirely on Phase 1 having supplied real logged history first.

Honest Note
No farm has subscribed yet. The conversion rate from free to paid, the marketplace's seller-side supply, and the predictive-analytics accuracy are all unproven assumptions, not measured figures — the same cold-start dependency named in value-proposition.json's Phase 2 section.

Use Case Prioritisation

Method

MoSCoW, weighted by whether the use case proves the core claim or merely uses it. Originally scoped to the Phase 3 hardware claim; UC-11 and UC-12 are must-have for Phase 1/2 on the same logic — without them, the app and premium layer are feature lists nobody asked for.

Must Have Rationale

UC-01, UC-02, UC-03, and UC-04 collectively prove the depth-plus-imaging claim for Phase 3. Without all four, the Phase 3 hardware layer is a camera with an expensive accessory. UC-11 proves the Phase 1 acquisition loop; UC-12 proves the Phase 2 conversion loop. Without those two, the phases above them have nothing to build on.

The Demo Use Case

UC-02 for Phase 3 — it shows the probe calibrating the camera in a single flight, which is the only thing about that layer a competitor cannot trivially copy. UC-11 for Phase 1 — it shows a farmer with nothing getting something for free, in one sitting.

The Revenue Use Case

UC-09 and the aeration side of UC-01 for Phase 3. UC-12 for Phase 2. All are boring, checkable, and pay for themselves while the more exciting claims in each phase are still being proven.
