Technology Readiness — Where We Actually Are

TRL progression with gate criteria, not calendar dates

chart-tech-readiness ↗Projection raw JSON ↗
nasa-trl

The development roadmap as a TRL progression, gated by evidence rather than dates. The company is at TRL 3 today — analytical proof of concept, nothing flown. Each step up is unlocked by a specific piece of evidence, and two gates carry explicit KILL criteria. Rendering this against evidence-gates rather than a Gantt calendar is deliberate: a hardware timeline stated in dates the team cannot honour destroys credibility.

Note

Why This Framing

Investors and thesis committees both need to know the honest maturity. The render is persuasive and the spec is detailed, which together can imply more readiness than exists. This chart's job is to make TRL 3 unmistakable. See company-profile.json#/the_honest_position and milestones-roadmap.json.
