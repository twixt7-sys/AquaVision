ℹTHE DELIBERATE EMPTINESS OF THIS FILE
Statement:

This file is empty of traction because there is none, and that emptiness is a deliberate act of integrity, not an oversight.

The Temptation Being Resisted:

It would be trivial to populate this file with invented pilot agreements, fabricated letters of intent, imaginary customer counts, or a 'we are in discussions with' list that overstates a single polite email. Many pitch decks do exactly this.

Why We Refuse:

A pitch that invents traction is fraud, and it is the specific kind of fraud that diligence is designed to catch. A single fabricated LOI that an investor calls to verify ends the raise and the reputation. A pitch that shows honest zeros against a rigorous plan survives diligence and builds the trust that the unverifiable later claims depend on. See funding-ask.json#/the_counterintuitive_bet.

The Principle:

Fill this file only from reality. Every field below is a real category of traction, currently and honestly at zero or 'not started'. When real traction arrives, it goes here, dated and verifiable.

The Milestones That Will Constitute Real Traction

Note

What to put here as it actually happens. Each is a genuine de-risking event, ordered by the risk it removes.

In Order
  • Phase 0: 25+ documented farmer interviews with a written synthesis — including operators who said no and why.
  • Phase 0: a signed academic validation MOU.
  • Phase 0: a documented BVLOS pathway from CAAP engagement.
  • Phase 1: winch 1000-cycle reliability demonstrated.
  • Phase 2: profile-vs-reference-sonde agreement demonstrated — the moment the core claim becomes true.
  • Phase 3: a full-season validation dataset, peer-reviewed by the academic partner.
  • Phase 3: a documented case of a warning leading to a useful intervention.
  • Phase 4: the first paying customer with renewal intent.
  • Phase 4: measured (not modeled) unit economics.
  • Phase 4: BVLOS authorisation obtained.
The Most Important One

The profile-vs-reference-sonde agreement (Phase 2). Everything before it is preparation; everything after it is building on a proven core. It is the single traction milestone that most changes the company's fundability, because it converts the central technical claim from 'plausible' to 'demonstrated'.

How To Present No Traction

The Pitch

'We have no traction, and here is why that is the honest and correct state for a company at this stage: we have deliberately not spent on hardware before proving the concept can be built and that farmers will pay. Here is the small, cheap first phase that produces our first real traction — 25 farmer interviews, a validation MOU, and the payload arithmetic — and here are the kill criteria under which we would stop. Fund that, and watch us produce evidence.'

Why This Works

It reframes the absence of traction from a weakness into evidence of capital discipline. It is far stronger than a padded traction slide, and it is true. The investors who reject it in favour of a company with fabricated LOIs are selecting for dishonesty, and they are not the investors this company wants.
