Biosecurity Protocol

biosecurity-protocol v1.0.0 updated 2026-07-17 ◎Target ◆Confidence: medium raw JSON ↗
worldbank-disease-risk-2014

Why This Is A First Class Document

A drone whose probe visits pond A and then pond B is a pathogen vector with rotors. This is the single strongest technical objection to the multi-site concept, and it is answered here — in a protocol backed by hardware — or it is not answered at all. Aquaculture disease losses run to roughly USD 6 billion annually; a monitoring product that spreads disease between the farms it monitors would be a catastrophic own-goal.

Citation Ids

  • worldbank-disease-risk-2014

The Threat Model

Vectors
VectorRiskWhy
The sondehighIt is immersed in the culture water of every site it visits. The primary vector.
The tetherhighAlso immersed, over its full deployed length, and routinely forgotten. Wiping the sonde while reeling in a contaminated tether accomplishes nothing.
The airframemediumContacts spray and aerosol. Full decontamination at operational cadence is impractical, so residual risk is non-zero and must be disclosed.
Dispensed materialmediumCross-contamination of hoppers and lines between substances and sites.
Field staff and equipmentmediumThe oldest vector in aquaculture — boots, hands, tools. Not unique to us, but we add to the traffic.
Pathogens of Concern

Species-dependent. Viral (e.g., WSSV in shrimp), bacterial, parasitic, fungal. The specific decontaminant and contact time must be chosen against the pathogens relevant to the region and species — this requires an aquatic animal health specialist, not a founder's judgement.

The Control Hierarchy

Note

Ordered from strongest to weakest. Rely on the strongest; do not lean on the weakest.

Controls
Control
Single-site sonde assignment
Strength
primary — eliminate the vector rather than clean it
Detail

Each site has its own dedicated sonde. The drone travels between sites; the probe does not. A sonde is logged to a site and a sonde appearing at the wrong site is an incident.

Cost

A sonde per site is a real capital line (the sonde is the most expensive component — see bill-of-materials.json). It is far cheaper than an outbreak, and it is the reason this cost sits in the unit economics deliberately rather than being an oversight.

Requirement Id
REQ-HW-006
Enabling Requirement

Tool-free 60-second sonde swap. If swapping is a workshop job, single-site assignment fails in practice because nobody will do it.

Control
Dock decontamination cycle
Strength
secondary
Detail

Freshwater rinse plus a validated decontaminant, applied to sonde AND full tether length, before any inter-site movement.

Requirement Id
REQ-HW-007
Validation

Efficacy audited quarterly by an aquatic health specialist. A biosecurity claim needs periodic evidence, not just a written procedure.

Control
Movement logging and traceability
Strength
supporting
Detail

Every sonde's complete site-and-time history is recorded. After an outbreak, an epidemiological trace is possible.

Why It Matters Legally

If a farm has an outbreak and we visited, we WILL be asked whether we brought it. A complete movement log is the difference between demonstrating we did not and being unable to say. This is liability protection as much as biosecurity.

Control
Movement restrictions during declared outbreaks
Strength
supporting
Detail

A site under a disease declaration is quarantined from the shared-equipment rotation. Its dedicated sonde stays with it and is destroyed or terminally disinfected rather than returned to service.

Control
Airframe surface hygiene
Strength
weak — acknowledged residual
Detail

Practical surface cleaning between sites, accepting that full airframe decontamination at cadence is not feasible.

Honest Disclosure

This is the residual risk. The airframe contacts aerosol and cannot be fully decontaminated at operational tempo. We disclose this to customers rather than let them discover it. Overclaiming 'fully biosecure' here would be exactly the kind of dishonesty the brand voice prohibits.

The Design Consequences

Statement
Biosecurity is not a procedure layered on top of the hardware. It shaped the hardware.
Consequences
  • The tool-free rapid sonde swap (REQ-HW-006) exists because single-site assignment must be operationally trivial.
  • The dock decontamination station (docking-station.json) exists for this.
  • The tether rinse — not just the sonde rinse — is specified because the tether is the forgotten vector.
  • A per-site sonde is carried in the BOM and the unit economics deliberately, not by accident.
  • The sonde site-assignment field in the telemetry schema is a biosecurity control surfaced as data.
The Point

A repository that treated biosecurity as an operational footnote would have a lighter BOM and better-looking unit economics — and a product that spreads disease. The cost is paid deliberately here because the alternative is worse.

The Honest Position For the Pitch

What To Say

Yes, a multi-site probe drone is a disease-transmission risk. We treat it as a first-class hardware requirement: dedicated per-site sondes, full-tether decontamination, complete movement traceability, and an audited efficacy programme. We also disclose the residual airframe-surface risk rather than claim it away.

Why Volunteer It

A knowledgeable buyer or investor — especially anyone who has lived through an outbreak — will raise this objection. Having engineered the answer, and volunteering the residual limitation, converts the strongest objection against the concept into a demonstration of engineering seriousness. Waiting to be asked, and then improvising, does the opposite.
