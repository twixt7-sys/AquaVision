Telemetry & Data Schema

telemetry-schema v1.0.0 updated 2026-07-17 ◎Target raw JSON ↗

The wire and storage format for everything the OmniDrone produces. This is the contract between aircraft, dock, cloud, twin, and dashboard. Field definitions resolve against data-dictionary.json; do not duplicate semantics here.

Design Rules

  • UTC always, RFC 3339, millisecond precision. Local time is a presentation concern. Storing local time would destroy the diel analysis the first time a site crosses a DST or timezone boundary — and the diel analysis is the product.
  • Every measurement carries a QC flag at source. Unflagged data is untrusted data.
  • Every position carries a horizontal accuracy estimate. A position without an error bar cannot answer whether two profiles are at the same station.
  • Model outputs live in a separate object from measurements. Never the same array.
  • Records are append-only. A correction is a new record referencing the original.
  • A TAN reading without its pH and temperature partners at the same depth and instant is rejected at ingestion, not stored and patched later.

The Rule That Matters

Flag 3 (suspect) data is shown to humans and excluded from automated triggers. This split is deliberate: a human can weigh a suspect reading in context; an alert rule cannot, and a false alarm from a fouled sensor at 04:30 destroys trust in the whole system.

Ingestion Validation

Reject Rather Than Repair

Malformed records are rejected at the boundary and returned to the dock's error queue. They are NOT partially stored and patched later. A dataset containing quietly repaired records is a dataset nobody can defend in an audit or a thesis.

Mandatory Checks
  • Schema conformance.
  • Partner presence — TAN without pH and temp at the same depth and instant is rejected.
  • Temporal monotonicity within a profile.
  • Position accuracy present.
  • QC flags present on every channel.
  • Model records carry model_version.
  • Imagery concentration products carry an uncertainty field reference.
  • Dispensing records carry a human authoriser.
